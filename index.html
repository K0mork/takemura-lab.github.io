Overview

Welcome to the human sensing laboratory led by Dr. Kentaro Takemura. We focus on sensing technologies for user interfaces, robotics, and medical applications. Currently, we study vision-based human sensing technologies, such as eye-tracking and contact sensing. If you are interested in our research field, we welcome your participation, collaboration, and visits.

Main research topics

  • Eye-tracking method
  • Contact sensing
